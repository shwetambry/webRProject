Services - Solutions - What we do

Our technical persons are experts in their respective domains and strive to put all efforts to streamline the patent prosecution process.

We provide a wide range of patent, trademark, copyright, design and domain name filing, prosecution and litigation services to our clients. The major services provided by us include but are not limited to patent, trademark, design and domain name availability searches and viewpoint on the registration of the same; performing prior art searches and opinion on patentability; drafting provisional and complete specification of patent, filing of patents, freedom to operate (FTO) searches, preparing and prosecuting domestic and foreign trademark, patent, design and copyright registration applications including PCT National Phase Patent applications; response to office actions from the respective IP offices; conducting opposition proceedings to defend or oppose the grant of IP rights; initiating and defending infringement and passing off actions; drafting of license, assignment, and copyright agreements; prosecution of appeals and rectification/cancellation actions before the Intellectual Property Appellate Board; watching services; maintenance of intellectual property by docketing and renewal services.
